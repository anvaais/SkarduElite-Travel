Iconic Destinations from Skardu

Discover the most scenic drives in Gilgit-Baltistan. We recommend the best vehicles for each route.

📏 ~80 km · 3 hours

Deosai National Park

World's second highest plateau. Best explored in a 4x4 Jeep. Open June–October.

📏 ~32 km · 1 hour

Shangrila Resort

The "Heaven on Earth" with its famous heart-shaped lake. SUV or sedan recommended.

📏 ~103 km · 2.5 hours

Khaplu Palace & Valley

Ancient Chaqchan Mosque and the grand Khaplu Palace. Paved road, any vehicle works.

📏 ~9 km · 20 mins

Satpara Lake

Crystal-clear alpine lake minutes from Skardu city. Perfect for a quick getaway.

📏 ~32 km · 45 mins

Shigar Fort & Cold Desert

17th-century fort turned heritage hotel, and the magical Katpana Cold Desert nearby. Any vehicle works.
